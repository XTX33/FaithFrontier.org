## Case Overview

This case is an active § 1983 civil-rights lawsuit filed by Barber in the
District of New Jersey, Camden Vicinage. It arises from interactions with
Hamilton Township officers and Atlantic County law-enforcement and detention
officials.

The docket currently reflects the initial Complaint (ECF No. 1), IFP application,
proposed order, and the court’s Pro Se guidelines notice (ECF No. 2). Once the
court rules on IFP status and screening, additional orders and scheduling
directives will be added here and linked.
