  <h1>Privacy Policy</h1>
  <p class="lead">
    Faith Frontier Ecclesiastical Trust is committed to transparency and responsible stewardship of information.
    This policy outlines how we handle data, protect privacy, and manage public records.
  </p>

  <h2>Information Collection</h2>
  <p>
    This website is a public documentation portal. We do not collect personal information through forms,
    cookies, or tracking technologies. Standard web server logs may record:
  </p>
  <ul>
    <li>IP addresses (for security and analytics purposes)</li>
    <li>Browser type and operating system</li>
    <li>Pages accessed and time stamps</li>
    <li>Referring websites</li>
  </ul>
  <p>
    This information is used solely for site administration, security monitoring, and improving accessibility.
    We do not sell, share, or distribute this data to third parties.
  </p>

  <h2>Email Correspondence</h2>
  <p>
    If you contact us via email, your message and contact information are kept confidential unless you
    explicitly request otherwise. We may retain correspondence for record-keeping purposes but will not
    publish, share, or distribute your personal information without consent.
  </p>

  <h2>Public Records</h2>
  <p>
    Case documents, court filings, and other materials published on this site are obtained from public sources
    or created as part of legal proceedings. Information that is already part of the public record (such as
    court dockets, filings, and orders) may be published here for educational and transparency purposes.
  </p>
  <p>

    please contact us at <a href="mailto:DTB33@pm.me">DTB33@pm.me</a>.
  </p>

  </p>

  <h2>Children's Privacy</h2>
